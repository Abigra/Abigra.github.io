## Research topics:

#### Computer Vision for in situ Ecology

I develop computer-vision methods to detect and quantify coral reef fish behaviors in field, enabling scalable behavioral monitoring across an entire underwater ecosystem. My work combines light-weight deep learning models, active learning, and custom annotation pipelines.

#### The Scaling of Ecological Behaviors

I study how individual behaviors aggregate to ecosystem-scale patterns across spatial and temporal scales. To this end, I link hierarchical models and simulation experiments to link individual decision rules to population level dynamics. 

#### Collective Behaviour and Misinformation

I investigate how collective decision making processes in animal groups and information spread in social systems produce emergent patterns and maladaptive outcomes. Methods include agent-based models, comparative phylogenetic analyses, and empirical field studies; recent work examines behavioral diversification nd the ecolical consequences of misinformation.
