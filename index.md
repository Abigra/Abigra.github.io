I am a PhD candidate in Computational Biology at Cornell University. My research focuses on the intersections of ecology, behavior, and technology. I work on building computer vision workflows that enable the analysis of underwater ecological systems, coupling field work and computational work throughout my research. Ultimately, I am interested in understanding how behavior and the formation of groups influences the ecology of a system. I am also passionate about making science, research, and education more accessible to the community, and I participate in scientific out reach with local schools in the Ithaca area. Outside of the lab (and field) I love to bake, hike, and play water polo!
